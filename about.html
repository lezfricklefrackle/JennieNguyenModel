ABOUT

Hi, I'm Jennie Nguyen!

Born and raised in Germany, Stuttgart with Vietnamese roots and currently based in Bangkok. I am 28 years old and just graduated from Chulalongkorn University M.A.

I am building my portfolio through editorial, streetwear, fashion and film projects, though I am open to more as I want to explore every opportunity.

My modeling work focuses on:

- visual storytelling,

- creative collaborations,

- and developing a versatile portfolio across photography, music videos and independent productions.

Currently, I am collaborating on two short films, a music video and a documentary. Furthermore, I am collaborating with a few photographers.

Modelling is a passion that allows me to grow, to express creativity, step into new environments and connect with inspiring people from different artistic backgrounds. I look forward to working with creatives who share the same mindset!
